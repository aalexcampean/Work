Monstera Deliciosa

Sunlight

Thrives in bright to medium indirect light.
Not suited for intense, direct sun but can be acclimated to withstand it.

Water

Water every 1-2 weeks, allowing soil to dry out between waterings.
Expect to water more often in brighter light and less often in lower light.
Pro tip: Monsteras can benefit from filtered water or water left out overnight before using.

Humidity

Normal room humidity will do, but prefers humid conditions if possible.
Consider incorporating a fine-mist mister or humidifier to boost humidity level indoors.

Temperature

Most houseplants prefer temps in the 65°F-85°F range (18°C-30°C).
It’s best not to let it go below 60°F (15°C).

Soil

Use a well-draining potting mix.
Mix in ingredients such as perlite or lava rocks to increase soil aeration as need.

Aloe Vera

Sunlight

they have proper light. Aloe vera plants need bright light.
They do best in south or west-facing windows.

Water

Water aloe vera plants deeply, but infrequently.
The soil should feel moist after watering, but should be allowed to dry out to some extent.
Water your aloe plant about every 2-3 weeks in spring and summer and more sparingly during fall and winter.

Humidity

Normal room humidity will do, but prefers dry conditions if possible.
Consider placing it on a window next to a radiator during the cold months.

Temperature

Most houseplants prefer temps in the 65°F-85°F range (18°C-30°C).
It’s best not to let it go below 60°F (15°C).

Soil

A cactus potting soil mix or a regular potting soil that has been amended with additional perlite or building sand is best.
